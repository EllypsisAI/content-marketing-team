# Content Strategy

Platform strategy, posting cadence, competitive positioning, content flywheel, and the 90-day launch plan. This is the operational backbone the calendar and week skills use.

## Platform Hierarchy

LinkedIn is the entire game. Everything else is a satellite.

| Priority | Channel | Role | Time/week | Start |
|----------|---------|------|-----------|-------|
| **1** | **LinkedIn (personal profile)** | Primary hub. All original content starts here | 5-6 hrs | Now |
| **2** | **LinkedIn Newsletter** | Depth layer. Biweekly deep dives that feed post series | 1.5 hrs (avg) | Week 1 |
| **3** | Meetup speaking | Credibility accelerator. Content multiplier | 0.5 hrs (avg) | Month 2-3 |
| **4** | Podcast guesting | Borrowed audience. Zero production overhead | 0.5 hrs (avg) | Month 3-4 |
| **5** | YouTube (screen recordings) | Long-tail SEO. Proof of expertise | 1 hr (avg) | Month 4-6 |
| **6** | Substack | List ownership. Mirror of LinkedIn newsletter | 0.5 hrs | Month 6-9 |
| **7** | X | Heartbeat cross-post only. Not a content investment | <0.5 hrs | Whenever |

Total at full build (Phase 3): ~8-10 hrs/week. Starting investment (Phase 1): ~7 hrs/week.

## Competitive Positioning

### The white space

Nobody in Denmark is doing implementation-level content. Confirmed across LinkedIn, Reddit, X, YouTube, podcasts, Substack.

### Competitor map

| Layer | Player(s) | Lane | Threat |
|-------|----------|------|--------|
| Awareness | Anders Baek | Tells mainstream Denmark AI is coming | None. His audience is Ellypsis's upstream pipeline |
| Education | Julian Bent Singh | Teaches individuals to use AI tools | Low. Different buyer |
| Building | Ruben Juncher | Micro-SaaS, vibe coding | None. No enterprise relevance |
| Data infrastructure | Dennis Uhrskov Raagaard | AI-native data platforms | Low-medium. Genuine practitioner but locked in data lane |
| Institutional | Implement, EY, McKinsey DK, BCG, AI Denmark | Corporate reports, enterprise-scale | Low for content. Company pages get 1-2% engagement vs 7-8% for personal |

### The positioning statement

> "I implement AI with Danish companies. Here's what actually works."

Janteloven-compatible (describes activity, not status). Trust-building by design ("here's what actually works" implies honesty about what doesn't). Implementation-specific. SME-accessible.

### The model: Morten Munster

Scandinavia's leading voice on behavioral design. What he did: owned a specific intersection, created proprietary frameworks, published in Danish for intimacy, stayed concrete. Ellypsis mirrors this for AI implementation.

## The Content Flywheel

Client work IS the content. Every engagement produces:
- An implementation story (Pillar 1)
- A lesson about what worked or didn't (Pillar 1)
- A framework refinement (Pillar 2)
- A data point about Danish AI adoption reality (Pillar 3)

### Creation-repurposing flow

```
CLIENT WORK (the source)
    → LinkedIn Post (2-3x/week)
        → LinkedIn Newsletter (biweekly deep dive)
            → 3-5 post seeds (excerpts, pull quotes)
    → Meetup talk (2-3x/year) → LinkedIn post + newsletter + YouTube
    → Podcast guesting (4-6x/year) → LinkedIn post (pull quotes)
    → YouTube screen recording (monthly, month 4+) → YouTube Short
```

### Minimum viable content operation

| Activity | Frequency | Time |
|----------|-----------|------|
| Write 2 LinkedIn posts | Weekly | 2-3 hrs |
| Comment on target accounts | Daily (15-20 min) | 1.5 hrs/week |
| Write newsletter deep dive | Biweekly | 2-3 hrs (biweekly) |
| Respond to comments | Daily (10 min) | 1 hr/week |
| **Total Phase 1** | | **~7 hrs/week** |

This is the floor. Below this, the algorithm doesn't learn topic authority and growth stalls.

## 90-Day Launch Plan

### Days 1-30: Foundation

Objective: Establish topic authority. Start algorithm training. Make positioning visible.

| Week | Key actions |
|------|------------|
| 1 | First carousel: "The State of AI Adoption in Denmark" (Pillar 3). CEO scenario text post (Pillar 2). Set up LinkedIn Newsletter. Start daily commenting on target accounts |
| 2 | Framework carousel: "5 Questions to Ask Before Starting an AI Project" (Pillar 2). 50 personalized connection requests |
| 3 | First implementation reality post (Pillar 1, anonymized). First newsletter edition |
| 4 | Second implementation + framework posts. Apply to speak at AI Meetup Copenhagen |

**KPIs:** 8-10 posts, 1 newsletter, 50+ new connections, daily commenting cadence established.

### Days 31-60: Momentum

Objective: First newsletter cadence. Content series started. Engagement signals visible.

| Week | Key actions |
|------|------------|
| 5-6 | Launch "Implementing AI at a Danish Company" 3-part series (Pillar 1). "Augmentation Assessment" framework in newsletter #2 |
| 7-8 | "ChatRot" post (Pillar 2). Pitch AI Denmark podcast. First meetup talk if timing works |

**KPIs:** 16-22 total posts, 3-4 newsletters, comment rate trending toward 0.2%+, at least 1 post with 50+ engagements, 100+ targeted connections.

### Days 61-90: Authority Signals

Objective: Topic authority kicking in (78% visibility increase at 60+ days). Inbound signals appearing.

| Week | Key actions |
|------|------------|
| 9-10 | English "signature piece" for broader reach. Vertical-specific newsletter deep dive |
| 11-12 | First podcast appearance. Evaluate: what performed best? Double down. Assess YouTube readiness |

**KPIs at day 90:**

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Total posts | 30-35 |
| Newsletter subscribers | 200+ |
| Newsletter open rate | 35%+ |
| Comment rate | 0.3%+ |
| Inbound inquiries | 3-5 |
| Meetup talks | 1-2 |
| Podcast appearances | 1 |
| Named frameworks published | 2-3 |

## Strategy Revision Signals

| Signal | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Comment rate below 0.1% after 90 days | Content-audience mismatch | Revisit pillar angles. Test different formats |
| Competitor enters implementation lane | Window is closing | Accelerate. Increase frequency. Double down on frameworks |
| Newsletter open rate below 25% after 10 editions | Content-audience fit problem | Review topics. Survey subscribers |
| Zero inbound after 6 months | Funnel broken | Audit audience targeting, content specificity, CTA clarity |
| AI content becomes indistinguishable | Authenticity advantage erodes | Shift to video/audio. Double down on meetup speaking |
